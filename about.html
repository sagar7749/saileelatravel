About Us

Sit Back, Relax — Rajendra Bhai Will Handle the Road.

Namaste! I am Rajendra Bhai Mali.

Thirty years ago, I came to Gujarat and started my journey as a driver. For 21 years I drove trucks. After that I decided now it’s time to start my own business. I bought a second-hand Innova and began driving it myself. Step by step, with your trust and blessings, today I proudly run Sai Leela Tour & Travel, serving customers for the past 9 years with honesty and dedication.

From Gujarat to every corner of India, including Nepal and Bhutan, I’ve traveled thousands of kilometers and know the best routes, popular spots, and hidden gems. I treat every customer like family, which is why they always choose to travel with me again.

Today, my clean and well maintained fleet ensures a smooth and comfortable journey. Whether it’s a family vacation, corporate trip, or spiritual yatra, Sai Leela Tour & Travel is ready with professional drivers, safe travel, and warm hospitality.

When you sit in my car, I take full responsibility for your comfort, safety, and happiness. My only aim is to make your journey unforgettable. Whenever you plan your next trip, just call Rajendra Bhai — always ready.
